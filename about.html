"GOOD EATS" IS YOUR ONE STOP SHOP FOR ALL THINGS CONTENT CREATION AND NUTRITION CONSULTING FOR YOUR FOODIE PRODUCT!

We specialize in food and product imagery for commercial, editorial, digital and social media platforms making it easy for you to promote your product with mouth water photography, videos and delicious recipes. Check out our services for more information.

Founder Supriya Mohan has been developing recipes, imagery and nutrition articles for her own blog – Supriya's Good Eats – for over 5 years where she found her passion for photography and has since worked with clients all over Australia, and internationally to develop content for their product, shoot cookbooks and engage and grow their community on social media. Supriya has a Bachelor of Food science and nutrition and a Masters of Public Health nutrition so you can assure she will write the best evidence-based articles to support your product. With one of her proudest achievements to date developing and shooting recipes for the Jamie Oliver Food Revolution Website.
